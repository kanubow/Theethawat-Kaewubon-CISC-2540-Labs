For lab 4, I created a project featuring a barebones platform and a controllable capsule as the player. I tuned the DirectionalLight3D node souch that the shadows made by the light were noticeable. I also tried tuning the gravity and jump velocity such that the player was quick but not too quick. Finally, I positioned the camera so that the player and its shadows can be seen clearly. Overall, I learned how to make a simple 3D level in Godot, a controllable 3D player, and how to use Godot's 3D nodes.
